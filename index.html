About Me

I help businesses and e-commerce companies unlock their potential through data insights. With 3 years in Python and 1 year in SQL and Power BI, I excel in data collection, cleaning, transformation, preparation, and visualization. My goal is to provide actionable recommendations that drive success.

My skills include ensuring data accuracy and consistency, creating intuitive dashboards with Power BI, and expertise in data modeling and analytics solutions. I can help by creating descriptive visualizations for business overviews, providing data-driven decision-making solutions, implementing a data culture for continuous data collection and analysis, developing visualizations of metrics and KPIs using Jupyter and Power BI, analyzing customer data to identify patterns and preferences, and implementing multi-touch attribution models to quantify marketing ROI.
